Africa

South Africa

Capital Cities: Cape Town, Pretoria, and Bloemfontein

Population: 60.6 million people

Currency: Rand

I traveled to South Africa for my brother's wedding in January 2020, right before the pandemic hit. They got married at a vineyard. We climbed Table Mountain. We went on a safari and saw a lot of animals. There were so many impalas. They're called the "McDonalds of the Safari" because there were so many of them and they also had a 'M' marking on their butt like the McDonald's logo. Penguins were chillin' out.
